Reading gives you peace!

It's been not so long but a year back i started reading novels and other informative books.Always reading the course books and continuously focusing on syllabus gave me so much stress that it was tough for me to handle the stress. But reading was proven to be the best therapy ever to reduce my mental stress and also made me explore many new ideas. Simply by opening a book, you allow yourself to be invited into a literary world that distracts you from your daily stressors. Reading daily gives you a menal stability and it is essential for those who seek to rise above the ordinary. Reading for me was proven to be :-

Dreaming with open eyes !!
